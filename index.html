What is MetaDoc?

MetaDoc is an online platform for fetching, extracting,
saving and downloading metadata. It provides users ability to
get access to information that can be fetch from a file and also allows
users to download, save, export, and importantly protect their privacy
while sharing the files online. The platform can be used by photographers,
podcasters, videographers, authors and others.
